Grace Weng

Texas A&M University

Computer Science

About Me: Hello! My name is Grace Weng and I am a Junior Computer Science major with a minor in Mathematics. I am currently a Marketing and Merchandise Officer for Aggie Women in Computer Science. I spent most of my life in Southlake, TX which is a small suburb in the Dallas-Fort Worth Area. However, I have lived in a quite a few other places earlier on in my life. For example, here is a sequential list of everywhere I've lived: Troy, Michigan, Lansing, Michigan, Annapolis, Maryland, White Plains, New York, Baltimore, Maryland, Bedford, Texas, Grapevine, Texas, and then finally Southlake, Texas. Some of my hobbies outside of school include oil painting, drawing, cooking, and reading. I participated in VEX Robotics for over half of my high school career where I developed a love for technology, engineering, and leadership. Before the pandemic I participated in one long term volunteering program every summer. Some of those include serving as a navigator at the DFW International Airport, being a camp counselor at Chinese Youth Camp Dallas, and teaching English to underprivileged children in rural Taiwan. After the pandemic hit, I spent my summers working. Some of those include working at a small local Halal smoothie and deli shop, being a crew member at Sonic Drive-In, being a hostess at Whistle Britches, and then last year I had an internship at Pioneer Natural Resources where I was a Technology Solutions Intern. While my working experiences prior to last summer were tough, they taught me a lot of soft skills that are very necessary to succeed in other aspects of my life. In the future I hope to work at in a corporate environment doing a position that has to do with data engineering or Azure cloud. I may also potentially go to graduate school after I am a little more established in life.
